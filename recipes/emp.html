Empanadas

Empanadas are my favorite Puerto Rican dish!.

Ingredients:

  • Discs
  • Cooked protein or vegetable
  • Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Place a spoonful of filling onto each disc.
  2. Add cheese on top of the filling.
  3. Fold the disc in half and seal the edges by pressing with a fork.
  4. Bake or fry the empanadas until golden brown.
