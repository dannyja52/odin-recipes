Pizza

Pizza is a popular dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, olives, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare the pizza dough by mixing flour, water, yeast, and salt. Knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  5. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  6. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base.
  7. Add shredded mozzarella cheese and your choice of toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.).
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
  10. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
